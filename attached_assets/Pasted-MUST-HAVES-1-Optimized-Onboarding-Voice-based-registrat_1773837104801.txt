MUST-HAVES
1. Optimized Onboarding

Voice-based registration in Hindi/Tamil/Telugu
8+ Indian language support throughout the app
Simple 3-step signup: phone number → platform (Swiggy/Zomato/Zepto) → zone
KYC via Aadhaar + selfie verification
UPI ID collection for instant payouts

2. AI Risk Profiling

Worker scored based on: delivery zone, working hours, platform, weather history of their area, past claim history
Hyperlocal pincode-level risk mapping
Earnings baseline established from platform order data
Risk score updates weekly

3. Weekly Policy Creation

3 tiers: Basic / Standard / Premium (e.g. ₹29 / ₹49 / ₹79 per week)
Coverage amount scales with tier
Auto-renews every week via UPI mandate
Worker sees exactly what triggers a payout and how much they get

4. Parametric Claim Triggering (Zero-Touch)

3–5 automated triggers monitored in real-time:

AQI crosses 300+ (severe pollution)
Rainfall exceeds 50mm in 3 hours
Temperature crosses 45°C
Government-declared curfew/strike in zone
Flood alert issued for pincode


No claim filing needed — system detects and initiates automatically

5. Payout Processing

Instant UPI transfer within minutes of trigger
SMS + app notification with payout reason
Full transaction history in app
Payout amount = AI-estimated lost earnings for that disruption window

6. Analytics Dashboard (Insurer Side)

Live map of active triggers across cities
Claims volume, payout amounts, fraud flags
Zone-wise risk heatmap
Worker retention and streak metrics
Premium pool health (collected vs paid out)


INNOVATIONS
7. Live Trigger Dashboard (Worker Side)

Worker sees real-time AQI, rainfall, temperature for their zone
Progress bar showing how close conditions are to triggering a payout
"You are 12mm of rain away from a payout" style display

8. Explainable AI

After every trigger (or non-trigger), worker gets plain language explanation
e.g. "Rain was 38mm in your zone — payout needed 50mm. No payout this time."
e.g. "AQI hit 320 in Andheri East. ₹180 credited to your UPI."

9. Pre-emptive Alerts

2–3 hour advance warning before predicted trigger event
"Heavy rain expected in your zone at 4pm. Consider finishing early."
Helps worker plan their day around disruptions

10. Earnings Prediction Model

AI calculates what worker would have earned during disruption window
Based on: their personal delivery history, day of week, time of day, zone demand patterns
Payout = predicted lost earnings (capped at policy limit)

11. Peer Comparison Fraud Detection

GPS data cross-checked across all workers in same zone
If worker claims but GPS shows they were active — flagged
If 80% of zone workers claim but only 20% were GPS-inactive — bulk fraud alert
Duplicate claim prevention across multiple platforms

12. Streak Rewards

Workers who go X weeks without claiming get premium discount
Encourages enrollment continuity
Displayed as a streak counter in the app (like Duolingo)

13. Micro Top-Ups

On days with predicted extreme events (cyclone warning, red alert rain)
Worker can buy single-day extra coverage for ₹5–10
Available until 6am of that day

14. Savings Pool Model

% of unclaimed premiums pooled annually
At year end, enrolled workers get partial cashback
Creates loyalty and long-term retention

15. Group/Fleet Plans

Fleet owner registers all their riders in one flow
Bulk discount on weekly premiums
Fleet owner dashboard showing all rider coverage status
Single UPI debit for entire fleet weekly

16. Platform Integration

Swiggy/Zomato order data (simulated) used to verify earnings baseline
Actual orders during disruption window cross-checked
Prevents self-reported earnings inflation in claims
